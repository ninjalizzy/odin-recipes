Lemon Bars

Description

  • 1. all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 c. powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 6 Tbsp. melted butter
  • 1.5 c. powdered sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3/4 c. lemon juice + 1 Tbsp. zest
  • 3/4 c. all-purpose flour
  • powdered sugar for topping

Instructions

  1. Make shortcrust. Preheat oven to 325 F. In a large bowl, combine first four ingredients. Line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment paper and press mixture in. Bake for about 20 minutes until slightly golden.
  2. Make filling. Mix together remaining ingredients. Pour over baked crust. Bake for about 30 minutes until the center no longer jiggles.
  3. Cool, cut and serve. Let pan cool for 10 minutes before transferring to the refrigerator to cool completely. Slice evenly and remove from pan. Dust with powdered sugar and enjoy!
